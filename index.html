Residential Loan Programs for a Wide Range of Needs

Andrew Kashella can help borrowers explore every type of residential loan through Innovative Mortgage's network of over 100 lenders. More flexibility to match a loan to your goals, property type, income structure, and long-term strategy.

Conventional Loans

A strong option for many borrowers with solid credit and stable income. Flexible terms and competitive rates.

FHA Loans

Helpful for buyers who want flexible qualification guidelines and lower down payment options.

VA Loans

Built for eligible veterans, active-duty service members, and qualified military families.

USDA Loans

A valuable option for qualified buyers purchasing in eligible rural areas with favorable terms.

Jumbo Loans

Designed for higher-value home purchases that exceed standard conforming loan limits.

Refinance Loans

For homeowners who want to improve terms, access equity, lower payments, or restructure debt.

Bank Statement Loans

Helpful for self-employed borrowers who may not show income in a traditional way.

DSCR Loans

A common solution for real estate investors focused on property cash flow rather than personal income.

Non-QM Loans

Alternative options for borrowers with more complex financial profiles or unique income scenarios.

Renovation Loans

Financing options for buyers or homeowners planning improvements and upgrades.

First-Time Buyer Loans

About Andrew

Meet Andrew Kashella

Andrew Kashella helps clients navigate the mortgage process with a clear, personalized approach. As a mortgage loan officer with Innovative Mortgage, he gives borrowers access to over 100 lenders and a wide range of residential loan programs.

Whether a client is buying a first home, refinancing an existing mortgage, or investing in residential real estate, Andrew focuses on finding practical solutions that fit the borrower's goals.

No two borrowers are exactly alike. Some clients need flexibility. Some need speed. Some need help understanding the basics, while others are comparing advanced loan scenarios. Andrew's role is to help borrowers sort through the options and make informed choices with confidence.
